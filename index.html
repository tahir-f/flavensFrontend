Our Story

Flaven's was born from a love of nature's beauty and the art of fine dining. Our founder, Chef Isabella Flaven, grew up in the countryside surrounded by lavender fields and rose gardens, which inspired her unique culinary vision.

After training at some of the world's finest restaurants, Chef Isabella returned to her roots to create a dining experience that celebrates the harmony between food and nature. Every dish at Flaven's tells a story of seasonality, sustainability, and artistic expression.

Today, our team of passionate chefs and floral designers work together to create unforgettable dining experiences that engage all the senses.
